About Me

Hello! I'm Jachin Lee, a Secondary 4 student with a passion for healthcare and a dream of becoming a nurse at Nanyang Polytechnic.

Living with Type 1 Diabetes since I was two years old has allowed me to experience healthcare from a patient's perspective. Throughout my journey, I have been inspired by nurses who showed compassion, patience and professionalism. Their care motivates me to become someone who can make a positive difference in the lives of others.

My Core Values

💙 Resilience

Growing up with diabetes has taught me perseverance. Every challenge has made me stronger and more determined to achieve my goals.

🤝 Integrity

I believe honesty, responsibility and trust are the foundation of nursing. I strive to always do what is right and treat everyone with respect.

Why Nursing?

Healthcare has been a significant part of my life. Through my own experiences, I have learned that nurses do more than provide treatment—they offer comfort, reassurance and hope. I aspire to become a compassionate nurse who can positively impact patients and their families while continually learning and growing in the healthcare profession.
